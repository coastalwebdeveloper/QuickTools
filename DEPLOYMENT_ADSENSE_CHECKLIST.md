## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### If Content Isn't Indexed
- Check robots.txt isn't blocking pages
- Verify sitemap includes new pages
- Manually request indexing in Search Console
- Check for technical SEO issues

### If Engagement Is Low
- Review content quality and readability
- Improve internal linking
- Add more visual elements
- Enhance page load speed
- Promote content on social media

### If AdSense Rejects Again
- Request specific feedback from AdSense support
- Compare your site to approved similar sites
- Consider hiring an AdSense consultant
- Focus on building more high-quality content
- Ensure perfect policy compliance
